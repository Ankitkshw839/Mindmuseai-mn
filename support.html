Frequently Asked Questions

What is MindMuseAI and how does it work?
MindMuseAI is an AI-powered mental wellness platform that uses conversational AI to provide support, track your emotional health, and offer personalized resources. Our AI chatbot engages in supportive conversations using natural language processing, tracks your mood patterns, and recommends evidence-based strategies for improving mental wellbeing. It also connects you with licensed therapists when more professional support is needed.
Is my data secure and private?
Yes, we take your privacy very seriously. All conversations with MindMuseAI are encrypted and secure. Your personal information is never sold to third parties. We comply with HIPAA regulations and industry best practices for data security. You can review our detailed privacy policy to learn more about how we protect your data.
How do I schedule a session with a therapist?
To schedule a therapy session, go to the "Appointments" page and click on "New Appointment." You can select from available therapists, choose a convenient date and time, and book your session. We offer both video and text-based therapy sessions. You'll receive a confirmation email with details and reminders before your scheduled session.
Can MindMuseAI replace traditional therapy?
MindMuseAI is designed to be a supplement to, not a replacement for, traditional therapy. While our AI can provide support, track your progress, and offer evidence-based strategies, it doesn't replace the personalized care of a licensed mental health professional. We encourage users to utilize our platform alongside professional therapy when needed and offer seamless connections to licensed therapists through our platform.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, and some insurance plans. For therapy sessions, we can provide superbills that you can submit to your insurance for potential reimbursement. Check your plan details or contact your insurance provider to verify coverage for mental health services.
